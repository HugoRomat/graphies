Star wars network



Graphies archive of this visualization.
Network of Star Wars characters that have talked with each other in episodes 1-9. Node size is proportional to the number of times a character appears is mapped on the color and the size. Main characters are represented with their pictures, and couple Anakin-Padme is emphasized with a custom red heart link. (JSON dataset: Interactions between StarWars characters)
